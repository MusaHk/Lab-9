The course on Information Communication Technologies (ICT) provides a comprehensive overview of the tools and systems that facilitate communication and information sharing in today's digital world. Students will explore the fundamental concepts of ICT, including hardware, software, networks, and the internet, while examining their applications in various sectors such as education, business, and healthcare. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, enabling students to develop proficiency in using various communication technologies, managing information systems, and understanding the social implications of technology. Through hands-on projects and real-world case studies, learners will gain insights into the evolving landscape of ICT and its role in driving innovation and connectivity.
